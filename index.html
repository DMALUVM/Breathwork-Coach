Daily Breathwork Routine

Your guide to feeling better, boosting HRV, reducing stress, and personal growth. Practice daily!

Morning: Energize and Set Intentions (5-10 min)

1. Kapalabhati Pranayama (2-3 min)

Sit upright. Inhale deeply, then exhale in rapid bursts (20-30 pumps). Repeat 3-5 rounds.

00:00

2. Coherent Breathing (3-5 min)

Inhale 5s, exhale 5s. Visualize compassion.

00:00

End with intention: "Today, I breathe in peace and exhale stress."

Midday: Stress Alleviation (5-10 min)

1. Box Breathing (2-3 min)

Inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s. Repeat 5-10 times.

00:00

2. Cyclic Sighing (3-5 min)

Deep inhale + short inhale, long sigh exhale. Repeat 5-10 cycles.

00:00

Evening: Wind Down and Reflect (5-10 min)

1. Nadi Shodhana (2-3 min)

Alternate nostril breathing: Inhale left 4s, exhale right 4s, etc. 5-10 cycles.

00:00

2. 4-7-8 with Bhramari (3-5 min)

Inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale hum 8s. Repeat 4-8 times.

00:00

End with reflection: Journal one thing you're grateful for.
